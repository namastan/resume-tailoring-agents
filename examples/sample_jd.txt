Senior Product Manager, Growth — Acme SaaS Co.

About the Role

We're looking for a Senior PM to own the growth loop at Acme. This role sits at the
intersection of product, data, and go-to-market. You'll be responsible for activation,
retention, and expansion across our self-serve and assisted-sales motions.

This is not a feature factory role. We're looking for someone who can define the growth
model, run structured experiments, and ship changes that compound over time.

What You'll Do

- Own the full activation funnel from signup through first value moment
- Define and track north star and input metrics for the growth team
- Run A/B and multivariate experiments across the onboarding and upgrade flows
- Work with data engineering to build instrumentation and reporting pipelines
- Partner with marketing on acquisition channels and landing page optimization
- Collaborate with sales on product-led growth (PLG) to assisted-sales handoff

What We're Looking For

- 5+ years of PM experience with at least 2 in a growth or PLG-focused role
- Demonstrated ability to move activation or retention metrics with product changes
  (we'll ask for specific examples with numbers)
- Strong SQL skills — you can pull your own data without waiting for an analyst
- Experience running a structured experiment program (hypothesis → design → ship → learn)
- Familiarity with B2B SaaS metrics: ARR, NRR, payback period, LTV:CAC
- Experience with a self-serve or freemium business model
- Ability to write clear product specs and communicate tradeoffs to engineering and design

Nice to Have

- Experience with product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel, Heap)
- Exposure to pricing and packaging decisions
- Background in developer tools or technical B2B products
